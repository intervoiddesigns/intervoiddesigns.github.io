The Hanged Man

The Hanged Man, known as Sacrilegious on Tapas, is my full-length fantasy novel that has been in the works since 2017. Set in an universe of my own creation, elemental gods choose one of their followers to give their elemental powers. Chaos ensues. This novel is finished, but I continue to edit and perfect it. To read some of my 422 page epic, please visit Tapas!
